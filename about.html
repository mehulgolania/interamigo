About Us

  • InterAmigo, is a company that specializes in assisting all tourists who come to Miami and Orlando from South America with all of their shopping and vacation needs after they have booked all of their travel tickets. All of our services are designed to assist you in achieving all of the best deals for all of your shopping needs while also giving you peace of mind to make sure your vacation goes as smoothly as possible. Our top services include our InterAmigo address use which includes delivery to your hotel, personal shopping assistants that go with you while you shop to facilitate the process , ( while also allowing you to get more done ) sold out product retrieval and delivery which includes all the products you want that may not be available for purchase during your vacation, and product shipment which includes pick up and shipping of your product back to Brazil just in case there's no more space in your luggage. InterAmigo is more than a service we are an international friend that will go above and beyond exceptions to ensure that all of our customers are always happy.
