How We Work

How Its Work

Step 1

To begin the process simply register on our site or log in and you will receive the InterAmigo address which you will use for all of your shipping needs.

Step 2

Feel free to make any purchases you would like on your favourite websites and use our InterAmigo info when asked to input an address upon completion of the checkout process.

Step 3

Once your purchase is made you will then go back to our website,www.InterAmigo.com, purchase the adress use and product delivery service and also select a date and time for your delivery.

Step 4

After you have scheduled a time for the delivery of the product,you have chosen to ship to us, you will then be asked to make a small payment of $10.00. When the payment process on our website is complete you will receive a confirmation email to make sure everything is good to go.

Step 5

Simply wait for your product to arrive at your hotel and enjoy the rest of your vacation stress free with all of your new purchases.
